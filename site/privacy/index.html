Pocket Memory Pet

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

Summary

Pocket Memory Pet is local-first. The MVP does not require an account and does not send pet data to a server.

Data Stored On Device

  • Pet name.
  • Pet status values.
  • Care event history.
  • Diary entries.
  • Sound and notification preferences.

Data Not Collected In The MVP

  • Location.
  • Contacts.
  • Photos.
  • Microphone.
  • Camera.
  • Advertising identifiers.
  • Payment information.

Notifications

The app may ask for notification permission to show local reminders. Denying permission does not prevent use of the app.

Third Parties

The MVP does not include advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, cross-app tracking, third-party analytics, account sync, or cloud backup.

Data Deletion

Pet data is stored on the device. The app includes a local reset flow, and users can also remove local data by uninstalling the app.

Contact

For support, use the Pocket Memory Pet support page.
