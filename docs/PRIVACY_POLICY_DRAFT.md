# Privacy Policy Draft

Pocket Memory Pet MVP does not require an account and does not send pet data to a server.

## Data Stored On Device

- Pet name.
- Pet status values.
- Care event history.
- Diary entries.
- Settings such as sound and notification preferences.

## Data Not Collected In MVP

- Location.
- Contacts.
- Photos.
- Microphone.
- Camera.
- Advertising identifiers.
- Payment information.

## Notifications

The app may ask for notification permission to show local reminders. Denying permission does not prevent use of the app.
