## Full Description

Open a tiny toy from a forgotten desk drawer and meet your new pixel friend.

Pocket Memory Pet is a cozy retro digital pet app inspired by warm desk lights, pastel stationery, tiny beeps, and old pixel screens.

Feed your pet, play Jump Star, clean its room, help it sleep, and watch it grow over time. Every little moment becomes a diary entry, creating a soft nostalgic record of your tiny companion.

Features:

- Raise a tiny original pixel pet.
- Feed, play, clean, sleep, and care.
- Cozy LCD-style pixel screen.
- Three-button retro interaction.
- Jump Star mini-game.
- Local offline save.
- Growth diary.
- Gentle local reminders.
- No account required.
- No ads in the MVP version.

Pocket Memory Pet is an original retro digital pet app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any existing toy or game brand.
